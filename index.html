About Me

Co-founder of the Professional Networking App: NCrowd.

Ncrowd is a Start-Up SAAS Company. Ncrowd provides a unique platform for Event Planners that enhances their members networking experience and increases the value of their marketing platform for their sponsors. When an Event Planner joins Ncrowd and uploads their events, their members recieve multiple features that will increase their chances of making more valuable connections. Ncrowds Sponsorship Program provides a platform for event sponsors to become more visible and interactive with attendees.
